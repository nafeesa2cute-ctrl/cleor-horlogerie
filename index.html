Our Story

Founded in the heart of Paris, Cléor represents the pinnacle of French horological artistry. Each timepiece is a testament to centuries of craftsmanship, blending traditional techniques with contemporary design. Our watches are more than instruments of time; they are heirlooms that capture moments and transcend generations.

Inspired by the elegant architecture of Paris and the timeless beauty of French art, Cléor watches embody sophistication, precision, and an unwavering commitment to excellence. Every component is meticulously crafted by master watchmakers, ensuring unparalleled quality and performance.

The Craftsmanship

Precision Engineering

Each Cléor movement is assembled by hand by our master watchmakers in Switzerland. With over 200 individual components working in perfect harmony, our calibers represent the zenith of mechanical watchmaking.

Artisan Finishing

From the delicate guilloché patterns on the dial to the hand-beveled bridges, every surface receives meticulous attention. Our artisans employ techniques passed down through generations.

Material Excellence

We source only the finest materials: 18k gold, platinum, and aerospace-grade titanium. Each material is selected for its beauty, durability, and horological properties.

Quality Assurance

Every Cléor timepiece undergoes 300 hours of rigorous testing in extreme conditions to ensure it meets our exacting standards for precision, durability, and beauty.
